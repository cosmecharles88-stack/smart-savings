The High Cost of Inaction: Liability & Risk

Deferring maintenance is not a budget-saving move; it is a massive transfer of public funds into high-probability legal and safety liabilities.

⚖️ CRITICAL RISK

Trip Hazard Liability

Any vertical displacement over **1/4 inch** is a primary target for "slip and fall" litigation. Settlements for sidewalk injuries in Texas can exceed **$100,000** per incident when including medical, pain/suffering, and legal fees.

Cost of Litigation:

10x - 20x Repair Cost

♿ FEDERAL MANDATE

ADA Non-Compliance

Under ADA Title II, municipalities have a federal mandate to maintain accessible paths. Failure invites Department of Justice (DOJ) enforcement, federal fines, and court-supervised "Transition Plans" that dictate budget spending for years.

Exposure Profile:

Uncapped Fines

🌊 INFRASTRUCTURE DECAY

Ponding & Surface Failure

Standing water (ponding) causes accelerated asphalt stripping and subgrade saturation. In winter, freeze-thaw cycles on ponding areas create "black ice" traps, leading to vehicle damage claims and pedestrian injuries.

Asset Impact:

50% Life Cycle Loss
